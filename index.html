Why Choose Us?

VENTURA empowers hotels and inns, and tourism offices to manage and scale businesses in all areas of Iloilo.

Manage hotel/inn in one place

When updating hotel/inn details such as room availability, it will automatically update for travellers basis and connected Tourism Offices. No more worrying about submitting reports.

Track bookings across platforms

Manage all of your bookings from one place, whether tourist is from any part of the world. Bookings can be approved or canceled in your booking management as you desire.

Capture last-minute bookings

Booking management allows you to accept bookings around the clock. Since booking confirmation is on hotels and inns approval, you won't miss out on bookings from travelers in different time zones.

Make the software work for you

Every tourism office will be able to monitor each hotel/inn details and productivity. Tourism Offices can monitor overall hotels and inns in Iloilo and dive deep into reporting to help scale business.
